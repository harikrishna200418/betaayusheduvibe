Get In Touch With Our
Counsellors

Our counsellors use their experience and professional expertise to help you make the right choice. We take into consideration several factors, such as your specialisation, projected career options, preferences, personality and match them with universities which have the best faculty in your choice of courses.

Research

Do a bit of research beforehand. In the beginning it definitely feels overwhelming, but the best way to go about it is by eliminating things that go against your interest or choice. If you can narrow down to a list of the institutions or courses, or even what kind of career path that you have in mind, it is easier to figure out the best way to go about it.

Application

After selecting your preferred institution and course, it‚Äôs time to begin the application process. Your counsellor will guide you through every step, from organizing your documents and meeting the required criteria to submitting your application. We will be with you throughout the entire process, ensuring a smooth and successful application experience.

Financial Support

We offer complete support throughout the financial process, guiding you from understanding costs to securing education loans. Our consultancy partners with top lenders like Avanse, Credila, Auxilo, M Power, Prodigy, InCred, and nationalized banks including SBI, Canara Bank, and Bank of India, providing competitive rates and flexible repayment options to help you fund your studies with ease.

Practice Your English Skills

A number of institutions require English proficiency tests such as IELTS, PTE etc. We will provide you the necessary resources to help you clear the exam, but you need to put in work as well. Not only is the medium of instruction English, you would also need your language skills to navigate everyday life as well.

Getting Accepted

The wait for the acceptance letter can be nerve-wrecking but we will always be there for any moral support or if you need help in choosing the right option out of the multiple courses and schools you have been accepted into. Once all is set, we will guide you through the next steps ahead.

Student Visa

Simply enjoy the excitement of your acceptance and let us deal with the grunt work of getting your student visa done. We will streamline the entire process and help your sort, organise and apply so that you can get your student visa done without hassle.

Live Your Dream!

Our support does not stop at getting you into a university. Once we get your visa done and you are all set to go, we will be right by your side, with any help you might need, such as finding accommodation, SIM cards, money exchange, bank account, health insurance and more.
